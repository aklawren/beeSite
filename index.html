About

This is official site for the Mid-Valley Beekeepers Association, covering San Joaquin and Sacramento counties. We welcome all bee-loving folks from the honey enthusiast to seasoned beekeeper. If you're passionate about keeping the buzz alive, feel free to stop by our monthly meeting!

We meet the third Wednesday of each month at Richmaid Restaurant
